Stay

Luxury Tents & Bush Caravan

Secluded accommodation for couples and groups who want the Karoo all to themselves.

1 Bedroom Open Plan

Luxury Tent

A romantic queen-size bed furnished with percale linen, comfortable tent furniture and solar lighting for night-time reading. The stoep outside your tent has wooden furniture to relax and enjoy the scenery.

Attached to the tent is a bathroom with flush toilet and bath. The tent includes basic self-catering amenities, a 12-volt fridge/freezer, gas stove or outside boma-braai.

  • Queen-size bed
  • Bathroom amenities
  • Private boma-braai
  • Kitchen amenities
  • Solar lights
  • Wildlife views

Caravan Site

Exclusive Bush Caravan Camping Site

A secluded and exclusive bush-caravan site for a group of friends and family. Facilities include shaded area, luxury bathroom with bath, open-air shower, 12-volt lights, dishwash area, small boma and separate fireplace.

Maximum 3 bush caravans and 12 guests. No standard caravans or tents — bush wagons only. Please contact us directly for rates and bookings.
