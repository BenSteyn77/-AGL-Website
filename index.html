Welcome to

An African
sanctuary unlike
any other

Nestled in the heart of the Little Karoo, African Game Lodge is a place where time slows, the stars hold court, and the land tells ancient stories. Just 2.5 hours from Cape Town along the legendary Route 62, a 30-kilometre dirt road leads you deeper into the wilderness — and further from everything ordinary.

Completely off the grid and powered entirely by solar energy, the lodge sits in harmony with one of the world's most biodiverse landscapes — the Cape Floral Kingdom. Every detail here has been designed to bring you closer to Africa.

Adventures

Game Drives

Morning and sunset drives through the reserve. Spot eland, kudu, wildebeest, springbok and more in their natural habitat.

Hiking Trails

Five marked trails ranging from a gentle afternoon stroll to a full-day mountain hike through kloofs, fossils and Baboon Caves.

Mountain Biking

Explore endless jeep tracks and scenic routes. Cycle through to Montagu or take on the wilderness trails within the reserve.

Getting Here

Your journey
begins in Montagu

Travel north along South Africa's legendary Route 62 — through mountain passes, wine farms and fragrant orchards — until you reach the award-winning town of Montagu. Then a 30-kilometre dirt road leads you home.

  • 2.5 hours from Cape Town via Route 62
  • 30 km dirt road from Montagu (45 min)
  • GPS: 33º43'23.70"S / 20º21'07.84"E
  • Satellite phone at reception for emergencies
  • Wi-Fi in main lodge · Solar powered
