Our Story

Our Story, Written in the Landscape

Set among vast valleys and mountains, our story is one of conservation, simplicity and timeless Karoo hospitality.

Welcome to

African Game Lodge

A harmonious union of Africa's wild heart and the timeless charm of the Little Karoo awaits you at our pristine, secluded lodge — completely off the grid, yet full of life.

From Montagu, a 30-kilometre dirt road leads you deeper into the heart of the Karoo, to the gates of African Game Lodge. Every moment of the journey hints at the magic that awaits.

Here, on our 4,500-hectare private game reserve, time slows down. Watch the sun dip below the horizon, fire up a braai under a star-strewn sky, curl up with a book, or simply breathe in the stillness.

The land carries stories of past inhabitants and ancient geological events, while our off-grid solar approach keeps the lodge connected to the future of careful conservation.
