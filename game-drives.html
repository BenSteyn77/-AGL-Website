Activities

Game Drives

Experience nature up close on a morning or sunset drive.

Join us for a morning or sunset drive to explore the far reaches of our reserve. A leisurely drive enjoying fresh outdoors and looking out for antelope species roaming the reserve.

The drive takes you along the Red House Trail where many animals gather, then into the mountains to see Cape Folding Mountain formations and a dam teeming with wildlife. Refreshments and a light snack are provided.

Drives

We offer morning, afternoon and combo drives. Please contact us for more information.

Self-drives are no longer available due to the impact on the environment.
