Game Drives

Experience nature, up close

Join us for a morning or sunset drive to explore the far reaches of our reserve. A leisurely drive enjoying the fresh outdoors and looking out for the various antelope species roaming the reserve.

The drive takes you along the Red House Trail where many animals gather, with a brief stop at the ruins of the first settlers to the valley. Thereafter we head into the mountains to see examples of the Cape Folding Mountains and wildlife-rich water points.

Self-drives are no longer available due to the impact on the environment.
