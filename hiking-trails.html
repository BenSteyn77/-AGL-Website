Activities

Hiking Trails

Self-hikes across kloofs, fossil beds, plains and viewpoints.

Whether you are up for a short stroll or an all-day hike, African Game Lodge has kilometres of wilderness to safely explore. No Big 5 means you are free to hike in safety while enjoying wildlife along the way.

Slagkloof Trail

All-day trail for medium-fit hikers through mountains, kloofs, riverbeds and succulent Karoo biomes.

Gannaleegte Trail

A short trail suitable for children, with riverbeds, kloofs and zoophycus fossils.

Gatskraal Trail

Half-day trail past Red House Ruins, birding areas, plains and wildlife.

Game Viewing & Birders Trail

A short loop with views over the pan and excellent late-afternoon wildlife sightings.

View Site Trail

A short late-afternoon walk with views of Montagu and mountain scenery.
