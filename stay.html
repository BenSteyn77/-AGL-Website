Accommodation

Choose from secluded luxury tents, spacious Karoo-style chalets and a bush caravan site for groups.

Stay at African Game Lodge

Experience Tailored Luxury

Guests who stay at African Game Lodge love the individual thatched Karoo-style chalets, designed to provide comfort and the opportunity to enjoy the spaciousness of the Karoo landscape.

Our luxury tents are situated about 2km from the main lodge, teeming with wildlife and birdlife. You are truly alone in the wilderness, tucked out of sight and surrounded by nature.

The lodge is completely off the grid. Chalets operate on 12-volt solar power. Laptops and devices may be charged in the main lodge, where Wi-Fi is available.
